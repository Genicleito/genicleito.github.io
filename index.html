Updated on: 19 Jan 2023

I'm a Computer Scientist, graduated from Federal University of Bahia (UFBa). I am currently a master's student of Programa de Pós-Graduação em Ciência da Computação (PGCOMP/UFBa) and I participate in the research group on Computational Intelligence and Optimization, with a research focus on Fuzzy Systems Based on Rules (FRBS).

I currently work as a Data Engineer. I'm responsible for orchestrating data pipelines, maintaining cloud architecture, creating SQL queries and dashboards for making available and presenting treated and structured data.

I worked as Data Engineer at Center for Integration of Data and Health Knowledge (Centro de Integração de Dados e Conhecimentos para Saúde) (CIDACS/Fiocruz), located in Salvador, State of Bahia, Brazil.

I worked as a monitor of the Robotics Workshop at Centro Juvenil De Ciência e Cultura (CJCC), between 2017 and 2018, teaching Robotics and Basic Concepts of Electronics. In 2014, I joined the group of Academic Computer Network Managers (GRACO/DCC/UFBa), at Computer Science Department, where I improved my knowledge with Network Administration and Linux Servers.

I'm a researcher in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Fuzzy Systems, since my graduation and also now in postgraduate studies. I currently work with analysis and engineering of large data sets.

I actively participate in the dissemination of knowledge, giving short courses and workshops on introduction to programming logic and robotics teaching, especially for high school students. I participated, voluntarily, as Communication Director of the Associação dos Estudantes Baixagrandenses (ASSEB), between 2015 and 2016.

I'm currently participating in a project called Rede Covida, a multidisciplinary and scientific collaboration project focused on the Covid-19 pandemic and a partnership between CIDACS/Fiocruz and the Federal University of Bahia (UFBA).

I'm currently working on my English.
