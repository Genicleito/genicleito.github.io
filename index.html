Updated on: 06 Oct 2019

I'm a Computer Scientist, graduated from Federal University of Bahia (UFBa), and Data Engineer at Centro de Integração de Dados e Conhecimentos para Saúde (CIDACS/Fiocruz) (Center for Integration of Data and Health Knowledge), located in Salvador, State of Bahia, Brazil. I'm also currently a special student of Programa de Pós-Graduação em Ciência da Computação (PGCOMP/UFBa).

I worked as a monitor of the Robotics Workshop at Centro Juvenil De Ciência e Cultura (CJCC), between 2017 and 2018, teaching Robotics and Basic Concepts of Electronics. In 2014, I joined the group of Gestores da Rede Academica de Computação (Academic Computer Network Managers) (DCC/UFBa) [http://wiki.dcc.ufba.br/Graco], at Computer Science Department, where I improved my knowledge with Network Administration and Linux Servers.

I'm a researcher in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Fuzzy Systems, since my graduation and also now in postgraduate studies. I currently work with analysis and engineering of large data sets.

I actively participate in the dissemination of knowledge, giving short courses and workshops on introduction to programming logic and robotics teaching, especially for high school students. I participated, voluntarily, as Communication Director of the Associação dos Estudantes Baixagrandenses (ASSEB), between 2015 and 2016.

I'm currently working on my English.
